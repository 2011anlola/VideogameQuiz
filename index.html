How to Play

Welcome to the Video game Quiz App!

In this web app, you can play a quiz where you'll be asked a question about a particular game, along with four possible answers. Only one of them is correct, so choose carefully!

If you select the correct answer, you'll move on to the next question until you reach the 10th one. But remember, if you choose a wrong answer, the game ends!

The quiz has a pool of 100 questions, and with 3 different difficulties: easy, moderate and hard.

There are a total of 10 questions. If you answer all of them correctly, you win!

Good Luck!
